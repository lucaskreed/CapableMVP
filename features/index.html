01 — Data sync

Your apps talk.
You don't have to.

Most fitness apps make you the middleware. You open four apps, copy numbers into a fifth, and still feel like you're missing something. CAPABLE flips that.

When you connect an app — Strava, Apple Health, WHOOP, Oura — we set up a persistent background sync. Data flows in automatically, deduplicated and normalized into a single timeline. You never touch it again.

  • ⚡
    Real-time for wearables. Oura, WHOOP, Garmin, and Fitbit push updates every few minutes via webhooks. Your data is never more than a few minutes stale.
  • 🔁
    Deduplicated across sources. Wore both a Garmin and recorded on Strava? We reconcile overlapping data so your totals are accurate, not doubled.
  • 📅
    Retroactive import. Connect an app today and we pull up to 2 years of history. Your full picture, not just from now.
  • 🔒
    Read-only access. We never write to your source apps. CAPABLE only reads — your Strava, your Apple Health, your data stays exactly as it was.

02 — Habits & streaks

Consistency is the
whole game.

Goals are easy to set. Showing up every day is the hard part. CAPABLE makes the invisible visible — your habits, your patterns, your momentum.

When you set a habit, we auto-complete it wherever possible from your synced data. Log a run on Strava and your "30 min movement" habit ticks itself. Log a meal in MyFitnessPal and hydration is marked. You build habits without building friction.

  • 🥈
    Auto-completion from synced data. Your apps already know what you did. We close the loop so you don't have to manually check off what already happened.
  • 🔥
    Streak protection nudges. We notify you before a streak dies — not after. A gentle push at 8pm if your habit isn't done yet.
  • 🏆
    Milestone unlocks. 7 days, 30 days, 100 days — each milestone is a real moment, shareable or private. Progress you can point to.

03 — Insights

Data you have.
Patterns you don't.

You're already generating a lot of data. The problem isn't collection — it's interpretation. Raw numbers don't tell you whether you're trending in the right direction or headed for a wall.

CAPABLE's insight engine looks across all your sources to surface patterns you'd never spot manually: sleep debt accumulating ahead of a bad workout week, recovery scores predicting injury risk, nutrition gaps correlating with low-energy days.

  • 📊
    Cross-source correlation. Your sleep score from Oura is compared against your Strava performance to surface real cause-and-effect, not guesses.
  • 📈
    Trend lines, not snapshots. Today's number means nothing alone. We show you 7-day, 30-day, and 90-day trend lines so you know if you're actually improving.
  • ⚠️
    Early warning flags. Resting heart rate creeping up? Training load spiking? We surface signals before they become problems.

04 — AI form analysis

A coach's eye.
In your pocket.

Good form isn't just about aesthetics — it's the difference between progress and injury. But most people never get objective feedback on how they move. They rely on a mirror, a guess, or nothing at all.

Record yourself doing a squat, push-up, deadlift, or any functional test. CAPABLE's computer vision model analyzes joint angles, movement symmetry, depth, and tempo — then gives you a score and specific cues to fix.

  • 🎥
    Works from a phone camera. No special equipment. Record a set from the side or front, and upload it in seconds. Analysis runs in under a minute.
  • 📈
    Tracks improvement over time. Re-test weekly and watch your score trend up. Visual overlays show exactly what changed between sessions.
  • 💬
    Specific, actionable cues. Not "try to go deeper." Instead: "knee tracking 12° inside neutral — cue external rotation before descent."

05 — Weekly reports

Your week,
summarized for you.

Every Sunday evening, CAPABLE compiles your week into a clean, readable summary — and sends it to your inbox. No dashboard required. No logging in to find out how you did.

The report covers everything: training load, sleep quality, habit compliance, nutrition averages, and a plain-language summary of what your trends mean going into the next week. It takes 90 seconds to read and replaces an hour of manual review.

  • 📅
    Automated, every week. Zero effort on your end. It arrives whether you remembered to check CAPABLE or not — accountability without friction.
  • 💬
    Plain language, not data dump. "Your sleep was down 45 minutes from your average — your Thursday run may have suffered because of it" is more useful than a chart.
  • 🎯
    One focus for next week. Every report ends with a single suggested focus area based on your trends. One thing to improve, not ten.

06 — Privacy & security

Your data.
Not ours to sell.

Health data is some of the most sensitive information that exists. We treat it that way. CAPABLE has never sold user data, has never run ads against your health profile, and never will.

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use read-only OAuth scopes wherever possible — we physically cannot write to your Strava or Apple Health even if we wanted to. You can export or delete everything at any time, with no hoops to jump through.

  • 🔒
    AES-256 encryption at rest. Your health data is encrypted before it ever touches our database. Keys are managed separately from data.
  • 👤
    HIPAA compliant. We maintain Business Associate Agreements with all data processors, and our infrastructure is audited quarterly.
  • 💾
    Full data export, anytime. Request a complete export of everything we hold about you. Receive it in 24 hours. No questions asked.
  • 🚫
    Zero ad targeting. We don't use your health data for advertising — not ours, not third parties'. No exceptions, no carve-outs.

FAQ

Questions people
actually ask.

Does connecting an app give CAPABLE write access?
No. We use read-only OAuth scopes for every integration. We can see your data but cannot modify, delete, or add anything to your source apps. Ever.
What happens if I disconnect an app?
The connection is revoked immediately. Historical data that was already synced stays in your CAPABLE timeline unless you choose to delete it — your choice.
How far back does historical import go?
Up to 2 years for most integrations. Some platforms limit what they expose via API — Strava and Apple Health typically give us full history going back several years.
How often does data sync?
Wearables that support webhooks (Oura, WHOOP, Garmin) update within minutes. App-based sources like Strava sync every 15–30 minutes, and on-demand via a manual refresh button.
Is form analysis available on all plans?
AI video form analysis is available on Individual Pro and all Coach plans. Free users can see example analyses but cannot submit their own videos.
Can I use CAPABLE without any connected apps?
Yes. You can manually log workouts, habits, and metrics directly in CAPABLE. Integrations make it effortless, but they're optional — not required to get value.
What formats does data export come in?
JSON for structured data, CSV for tabular data, and MP4 for any uploaded videos. Everything is bundled into a single zip file delivered to your email within 24 hours.
Does CAPABLE work offline?
The mobile app caches your recent data and habits locally, so you can log and view your dashboard without internet. Sync resumes automatically when you reconnect.
