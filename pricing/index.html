FAQ

Billing questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Plans run monthly and can be changed or canceled from billing settings.
Do you offer yearly billing?
Yes for coach, gym, and enterprise plans. Contact sales for annual discounts.
Do free users get AI?
AI workflows are part of Pro and above. Free users can still track and sync core data.
Can I migrate plans later?
Yes. You can move from individual to coach or gym plans without losing account history.
